Membership Level Information

Non-profit

Annual Fee: $100

Perks:

Access to directory listings

• Section for advertisement in Butlerville Days page

• Yearly business trainings

• Have a chance to be featured on the Cottonwood Heights website

Bronze

Annual Fee: $200

Perks - everything included in non-profit plus:

• Chance to get an advertisement on the Cottonwood Heights Facebook page

• Half-page advertisement in Butlerville Days

• Yearly ad displayed in Cottonwood Heights newspaper

Silver

Annual Fee: $400

Perks - everything included in non-profit and bronze plus:

• Yearly ad on Cottonwood Heights Facebook Page

• Full page advertisement in Butlerville days

• Small booth at Butlerville Days

• Special trainings for Silver and Gold businesses

Gold

Annual Fee: $1000

Perks - everything included in non-profit, bronze, and silver plus:

• Sponsorship graditude with events held in Cottonwood Heights

• Special trainings for Silver and Gold businesses

• Large booth at Butlerville Days

• Yearly luncheon with representatives for each Gold business
