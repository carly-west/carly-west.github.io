Welcome to Cottonwood Heights Chamber of Commerce

What do we do?

The Chamber of Commerce of Cottonwood Heights is an organization that promotes local businesses, and allows them to work together to promote local interest and community.

Why join?

All businesses located in Cottonwood Heights are implored to join our Chamber of Commerce because of the opportunity it provides their businesses to discuss and promote their product and services.
