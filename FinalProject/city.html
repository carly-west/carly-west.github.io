About Cottonwood Heights

History

Cottonwood Heights was incorporated on January 14, 2005. Before it was incorporated, the area of Cottonwood Heights was formerly a part of Salt Lake City.

Geography and Demographics

Located near Big and Little Cottonwood Canyons within the Wasatch Mountains, Cottonwood Heights has been given the nickname "City between the canyons". As of 2015, there were 34,234 people living in the area.

Upcoming Events

Butlerville Days

The Butlerville Days committee is excited to announce that this year’s event will be held July 19 – 24, with fun activities each day. We always strive to provide a safe and affordable family event for all of our residents. With the challenges of the past year, we are taking extra steps to keep everyone safe. To meet this goal, we have decided not to have the carnival or inflatables for this year. In their place, the committee is working on creating a full week of fun filled activities in the city.

Vaccination Information

Cottonwood Heights and it's volunteers have put in countless hours to try and protect it's citizens from Covid-19. The following is information on how to get your FREE COVID-19 vaccine:

  • SLCoHD Immunization Clinics
  • Community Vaccine Clinics
  • Private Provider

Important Reminders

  • Minors: People under 18 must be accompanied by a parent or guardian at their appointment.
  • Vaccine Brands: We cannot guarantee which brand of vaccine you will receive at your appointment; brand availability at each site varies and may change regularly.
  • We CANNOT vaccinate you if you have had passive antibody therapy to treat COVID within 90 days or are currently in isolation for COVID infection
